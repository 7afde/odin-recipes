Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it in a baking pan.
  3. Spread tomatoes over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top of the tomatoes.
  5. Place pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
