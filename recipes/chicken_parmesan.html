Chicken Parmesan

Description

This recipe is a classic Italian dish that combines the flavors of chicken and pasta with a crispy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon bay leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon zest

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl; mix well.
  3. Add wet ingredients to the bowl; mix until just combined.
  4. Divide the mixture into two equal parts.
  5. Roll each part into a ball using your hands.
  6. Place the balls onto ungreased baking sheets.
